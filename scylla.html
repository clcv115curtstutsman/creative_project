Scylla

Opting to brave the dangers of Scylla, you steer the ship towards the looming cliffs. The six-headed monster, Scylla, awaits in her lair, her serpentine necks coiled, ready to strike. As you pass, she lunges, her heads snatching countless of your companions of their ships. The loss is devastating, and your decision spares the sole The ship that you, Odysseus, and a few other crew members were on. Yet, the ship sails on, driven by the unyielding will to return home. But hunger now becomes your relentless enemy.
