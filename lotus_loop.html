Isle of the Lotus

As you consume the lotus, a blissful forgetfulness washes over you. The urgency of your journey fades into a dreamlike haze. Hours, or perhaps days, pass in this tranquil state until a firm hand shakes your shoulder. It's Odysseus, his face etched with concern and determination. He reminds you of your home, your purpose. With effort, you shake off the lotus's enchantment and stand, ready to make your choice again
