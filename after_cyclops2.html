Polyphemus's Curse

Once outside, you flee to the ship, the sounds of Polyphemus's anguished cries behind you. As you set sail, the Cyclops invokes the wrath of his father Poseidon to curse your crew. As the island fades into the distance, a heavy sense of foreboding settles over you. You've escaped the immediate danger, but the curse of a god's son might carry consequences far graver than any of you can foresee. Your ship sails off into the distance toward a another island.
