Death

Compelled by a mixture of curiosity and the hypnotic allure of Circe, you bring the goblet to your lips. The liquid is sweet, with a tang of the sea, and as it warms your throat, a strange lightheadedness envelops you. The world spins, colors and sounds begin to blend. You hear Circe's soft laughter as you fall into a deep trance. The enchantment is complete; you are now part of her collection, another lost soul trapped in animal form, wandering her gardens.
